Applications

Our magazine is powered by the curiosity of students like you. If you're looking for a way to use your skills to help others discover their passion for STEM, we'd love to have you on our team.

We're looking for passionate writers, creative designers, sharp editors and social media enthusiasts who want to help us share engaging content and build a vibrant community.

Content Writers

Write articles on biology, chemistry, physics, mathematics, computer science or academic advice. Share your knowledge and passion with other students!

  • Research and write engaging articles
  • Collaborate with our editing team
  • Contribute to our growing knowledge base

Editors

Have a sharp eye for detail? Help us ensure every article is accurate, clear and ready for publication.

  • Review article submissions
  • Provide constructive feedback
  • Ensure accuracy and clarity

Graphic Designers

Do you love bringing ideas to life with visuals? Help us create a stunning magazine by designing eye-catching layouts, graphics and covers.

  • Design magazine layouts and article graphics
  • Create visuals for our social media channels
  • Collaborate with writers and editors to bring articles to life

Social Media Managers

Help us expand our reach! Create and share content that connects with students and makes STEM more exciting.

  • Create engaging social media content
  • Manage our Instagram and other platforms
  • Engage with our community

Passion for Learning - A genuine interest in science, education or helping others learn

Creativity - A willingness to explore new ideas from a unique perspective

Communication Skills - The ability to express ideas clearly and work well with others

Problem-solving - Find new and unique ways to approach challenges

Portfolio Building - Add published work and team experience to your CV

Skill Development - Improve writing, editing, teamwork and leadership skills

Networking - Connect with like-minded students around the world

Experience - Get hands-on experience in a collaborative, creative environment

Support - Work closely with other students and get feedback to help you grow

Making a Difference - Help other students discover their passion for science
